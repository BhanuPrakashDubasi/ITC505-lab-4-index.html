Addendum: Stylistic Choices

Font Choices

I chose Arial, a clean and modern sans-serif font, to ensure readability and a professional appearance. This font is widely recognized and lends a contemporary feel to the resume.

Heading Sizes

The h1 tag is used for my name to emphasize its importance, while h2 tags are used for section titles to create a clear hierarchy. The h3 tags for subsections provide additional detail without overwhelming the reader, maintaining a clean structure.

Color Choices

I opted for a light grey background (#eaeaea) to reduce strain on the eyes while keeping the text dark (#333) for high contrast and legibility. The color choices for headings (blue, green, and purple) add vibrancy and help differentiate sections visually.

Margin and Padding Choices

Margins of 20px around the body create breathing space for the content, making it easier to read. Each section is given a bottom margin of 20px and padding of 15px, ensuring thereâ€™s enough space between sections and a comfortable area for text within each section.

HTML Abuse

Throughout the document, I ensured proper HTML semantics. The most significant adjustment made was using the h1 tag for my name instead of a h2 or h3 tag based solely on appearance. This maintains the document's structural integrity and enhances accessibility.
